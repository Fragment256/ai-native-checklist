## How to score

- **Yes** — retention, activation, and Day-7 / Day-30 targets are written down before launch, alongside a definition of what a "false positive" looks like for this product.
- **Partial** — some metrics are tracked but the targets and false-positive definition were not pre-committed.
- **No** — metrics were chosen after launch, or no targets exist.
