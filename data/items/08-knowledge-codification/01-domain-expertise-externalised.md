## How to score

- **Yes** — domain expertise (jargon, regulatory gotchas, edge cases, recurring workflow steps) is captured in a structured, AI-readable form. At least one workflow has been turned into a reusable skill that runs the same way every time.
- **Partial** — some knowledge has been captured but it is partial, stale, or not in a form the AI can act on.
- **No** — institutional knowledge lives only in the founder's head and undocumented workflows.
