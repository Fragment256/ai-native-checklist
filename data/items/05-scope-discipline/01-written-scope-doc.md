## How to score

- **Yes** — a scope document exists in the repo. It names what is in scope, what is explicitly out of scope, and the evidence threshold required to add a new feature.
- **Partial** — scope is roughly understood but not written down, or the "deliberately not" list is missing.
- **No** — no scope document exists. New features are added on enthusiasm.
