## How to score

- **Yes** — the founder can name, for at least three recurring tasks, which surface they use and why. Chat for quick exchanges, Cowork for connected-systems knowledge work, Code for shipping software.
- **Partial** — one surface is used for everything regardless of fit. Or surface choice is ad hoc rather than deliberate.
- **No** — only one surface is in use, or the distinction has not been considered.
