## How to score

- **Yes** — a current bottleneck map exists. Each item is categorised: automate / delegate / founder-only. At least one workflow has been moved off the founder's desk as a result.
- **Partial** — a list exists but isn't categorised, or nothing has been acted on since it was drawn.
- **No** — the founder cannot name what would stall if they were unavailable for a week.
