## How to score

- **Yes** — the problem hypothesis is written down, names a specific user with a specific situation, specifies a frequency or severity, and could be confirmed or disconfirmed by talking to ten of those users.
- **Partial** — a hypothesis exists but is generic, lives in someone's head, or has not been stress-tested against disconfirming evidence.
- **No** — the team is building against an observation ("people struggle with X"), not a hypothesis.
